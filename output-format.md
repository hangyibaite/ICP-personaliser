# ICP Output Format

Standardised output structure for the final ICP document. Use this after all interview rounds are complete and confirmed.

For synthesis rules to apply when writing, see `synthesis.md`.

---

## Output Rules

1. Lead with a one-line summary capturing the entire ICP.
2. Every section: heading + tight explanation. No filler, no theory.
3. Use the person's own language throughout.
4. Behavioral over aspirational — observable, verifiable terms.
5. Adaptive sections go where they naturally fit.
6. Drop irrelevant sections. No empty placeholders.
7. End with Quick Reference: Strong Fit / Not a Fit.

---

## Required Sections

The final document must include all of the following that are relevant. Drop only if the interview confirmed the section does not apply.

| Section | What it contains |
|---|---|
| THE ICP IN ONE LINE | Single sentence. Must work standalone — if someone reads only this, they know who the business is for. |
| WHAT WE ACTUALLY DO | Confirmed paragraph from Round 1. What the business literally delivers and what changes for the client. |
| THE PRIORITY FILTER | The single most important filter. One paragraph max. Format: "Before anything else, they must [X]." |
| HARD PREREQUISITES | Numbered list. Binary pass/fail. Each must be observable and verifiable in a short conversation. |
| WHAT THEY SELL & HOW | Offer structure, delivery method, sales mechanism, platforms. |
| THE MONEY | Price point(s), financial gates, client LTV if applicable. |
| THE PERSON | Full portrait paragraph (one specific human), demographics, beliefs & values, media diet & lifestyle. |
| HARD VIBE DEAL-BREAKERS | Specific observable behaviors that disqualify even if every other box is ticked. |
| ADAPTIVE SECTION(S) | Only if something emerged that was mentioned 3+ times or explicitly flagged as important. Titled based on what it covers. |
| NICHES | Two lists: niches served, hard no. |
| QUICK REFERENCE: FIT VS NOT A FIT | Two bullet lists compressing everything above. No new information. |

## Document Template

Single markdown file. Adapt structure to what is relevant.

```markdown
# Ideal Client Profile

Version 1 — [Date].

---

## THE ICP IN ONE LINE

[One sentence.]

---

## 1. WHAT WE ACTUALLY DO

[Confirmed paragraph.]

---

## 2. THE PRIORITY FILTER

[Single most important filter. One paragraph max.]

---

## 3. HARD PREREQUISITES

[Numbered list. Binary pass/fail. Observable.]

---

## 4. WHAT THEY SELL & HOW

[Offer structure, sales mechanism, platforms.]

---

## 5. THE MONEY

[Price points, financial gates, client LTV.]

---

## 6. THE PERSON

### The portrait
[Full paragraph — one specific human.]

### Demographics
[Age, gender, geography, language, life situation.]

### Beliefs & values
[Money, industry, worldview.]

### Media diet & lifestyle
[Who they follow, what they consume, outside-of-work life.]

---

## 7. HARD VIBE DEAL-BREAKERS

[Specific observable behaviors.]

---

## [8. ADAPTIVE SECTION — titled based on what emerged]

[Only if relevant.]

---

## 9. NICHES

### Niches we serve
[Bullet list.]

### Hard no
[Bullet list.]

---

## 10. QUICK REFERENCE: FIT VS NOT A FIT

### STRONG FIT
[Bullet list.]

### NOT A FIT
[Bullet list.]
```

## After Delivery

Once confirmed, offer:
1. "Want me to save this as a downloadable file?"
2. "If you want this as a permanent reference for future conversations, I can help set that up."

Do not push either. Just offer.
